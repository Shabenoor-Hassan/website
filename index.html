What is Global Warming?

Global warming refers to the long-term increase in Earth's average surface temperature due to human activities, primarily the burning of fossil fuels. This leads to melting ice caps, rising sea levels, extreme weather events, and disrupted ecosystems.

Solutions to Combat Global Warming

Renewable Energy

Transitioning to clean energy sources like solar, wind, and hydropower to reduce greenhouse gas emissions.

Energy Efficiency

Implementing energy-saving technologies in homes, buildings, and industries to cut down on energy consumption.

Reforestation

Planting trees as much as you can and restoring forests to absorb CO₂ from the atmosphere.

Carbon Capture

Investing in technology to capture and store carbon emissions directly from the atmosphere.
